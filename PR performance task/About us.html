Our History

Techton was founded after seeing many developers stuck in finding any progress in learning something new, a term we call "Tutorial Hell". Because of these issue, all 10 of us developers gathered to form a group to help developers who are stuck in finding any progress in their learnings

We chose the name TECHTON because it embodies the purpose of our project. The name is a blend of creativity, collaboration, and purpose. The word “TECH” is recognized in shorthand for technology, while “TON” was added because it gives a "Ton" of information, knowledge, and value. Together, "TECHTON" represents a space where technology and alat of potential come together. The name doesn't have a historical backstory but was born from our brainstorming, and random ideas in our brain.
