Arithmatic Operators



Arithmatic Operators Are ways for you to be able to perform simple calculation in your code.

Arithmatic operators:

  1. (Addition) + Adds two numbers. Example: 3 + 9 = 12
  2. (Subtraction) - Subtracts one number to another. Example: 10 - 5 = 5
  3. (Multiplication) * Multiplies two numbers. Example: 10 * 5 = 50
  4. (Division) / Divides one number by another. Example: 9 / 3 = 3
  5. (Modulus) % Gives you the remainder after a division. Example: 17 % 5 = 2 (because 17 divided by 5 leaves a remainder of 2)
