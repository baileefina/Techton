Horizontal rule and Line breaks



Horizontal Rule (hr tag) It creates a horizontal line across your webpage, separating conten sections and adding visual structure.

Line breaks (br tags) Is like a "new line" button in your word processor. It forces a line break, moving the following content to the next line.
