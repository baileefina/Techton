Text Format



text format helps convey elements effectively. Think of it like adding emphasis, style, and structure to your written words.

Here are different types of text formatting

  1. Bold (b) These tags make your text Bold. Giving it a stronger presesnce

  2. Italic (i) These tags makes your text Italic. Which is great for highlighting specific words.

  3. Underline (u) These tags makes your text have a Underline underneath it.

  4. Highlight (mark) These tags makes your text be Highlighted making it stand out.

  5. Strike (del) These tags makes your text Crossed out.

  6. Inserts (ins) These tags makes your text Insert new text
